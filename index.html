About Section


Born and raised in Alaska, the pioneer spirit was instilled in me from an early age, and I hold close the principles of adventure, resilience, and curiosity in my personal and professional life. My interest in software development was piqued when I watched a video tutorial on building websites, sending me down a rabbit hole of web and software development forums, blogs, podcasts, and ultimately enrolling in an intensive 3 month coding bootcamp. It still fascinates me to see lines of code being transposed into beautiful web pages with elegant UI experiences.

▷ I am a full stack software developer proficient in: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Vue 3, Nodejs, MongoDb, OOP, C#, .NET, MySQL

▷ I have a degree in media production with experience in harnessing and expressing ideas in a creative and engaging manner. I think outside of the box and try to approach problems and solutions from all angles.

▷ I am a US Navy veteran with the ability to think critically, objectively address problems as they arise, and ultimately achieve big-picture results on both individual and team levels. I am disciplined and intrinsically motivated to go above and beyond the minimum.

▷ I have experience in construction ground work and the power industry. I am not afraid to make a plan, coordinate a team, and get to work, all while under intense pressure, in high stakes situations, and often in less-than-ideal circumstances.

I sincerely enjoy learning news skills and working on a team driven by the same goals. I look forward to meeting both challenges and successes in my work with the same drive and adaptable attitude that defined my Alaskan upbringing, and I aspire for my work contributions to better myself and those around me.
